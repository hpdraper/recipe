Street Tacos

Street tacos are packed with flavor, made with tender steak, zesty lime, and topped with cilantro. Perfect for a quick meal!

  • Jump to Details

Street tacos are delicious and easy to make, filled with tender steak, lime, and topped with your favorite ingredients!

Try these other taco recipes: taco-stuffed avocados, Baja fish tacos, and ground beef tacos!

Details

Prep Time
10 minutes
Cook Time
10 minutes
Total Time
20 minutes
Author
Taco Cheesington
Servings
6 Tacos

Ingredients

  • Flank steak
  • Soy Sauce
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Lime
  • Minced garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Corn tortillas

Directions

  1. Cut and mix flank steak with soy sauce, Worcestershire, lime, cilantro, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate steak for 1-2 hours.
  3. Cook steak in a skillet for 5-7 minutes or until no longer pink.
  4. Add steak to tortillas with desired toppings.
